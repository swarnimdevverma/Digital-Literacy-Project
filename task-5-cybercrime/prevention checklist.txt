Stay Safe Online Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages
- Always verify the sender before sharing any personal information
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts
- Regularly update your apps and devices for security patches
- Never share OTPs, PINs, or banking details with anyone
- Double-check UPI IDs before making any payment
- Avoid scanning unknown QR codes for payments
- Use official apps and websites for financial transactions

Reporting Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
